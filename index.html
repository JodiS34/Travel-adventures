Why Tulum Mexico?

Tulum is the city of beauty Mayan history

As recently as the early 1990s, Tulum was a quiet village 2 km (1.5 mi) from the archaeological site, and tourism outside of the ruins was limited to a few small shops and simple cabanas on the beach. As of the 2020 census, population of Tulum municipality has grown to 46,721 permanent inhabitants with the addition of a number of residential developments in the jungle areas nearest Tulum's downtown.

With the rapid increase in tourism, vacation rentals, small hotels and hostels, as well as restaurants and bars, the town is getting more and more popular. Grocery stores, boutiques, bicycle rentals, gyms, tour operators, banks, ATMs, internet cafes, and various other commercial stores are available in Tulum town. Spanish Language Schools are popular in Tulum

Onyx Tulum

Onyx Tulum Restaurant Bar is popular for its energetic and festive atmosphere, with live music and performances that many travelers enjoy, though some find the music a bit loud. The wait time is generally short, and a complimentary drink may be offered. Guests frequently praise the delicious, fresh food and generous portions, though prices and value receive mixed reviews. Service earns rave reviews for attentiveness and friendliness, despite occasional concerns about billing practices.
